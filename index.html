NeuO In-Spire®

Resuscitator Automation System

An electro-mechanical device to enhance the utility of the existing Bag valve mask and automate the same.

Automate a premium bag valve mask, by sensing the patient's breathing pattern and pump the air+O2 accordingly.

Our solution theoretically and statistically saves more lives compared to a patient under bag valve mask operated by an untrained attender.

Product Readiness & Specs

Certification: IEC 60601

IPR Patent no: 202141007307

IPR Copyright no: 4799095

Major Settings:

  • Breaths per minute (BPM): 15, 20, 25, 30
  • Tidal volume: Low (200-250 ml), Medium (250-350 ml), High (450-550 ml)
  • PEEP valve: Yes
  • Oxygen Input: Up to 15 LPM
  • Mode: Low, medium, high, assist control low*, assist control medium*, assist control high*
  • Alarm: During no pressure, During battery mode, Serious injury
  • Pressure relief valve: Yes, 40 cm of Water (safety lock)
  • Battery Backup: Minimum 2 hours with inverter

Market & Impact

Socio-Economic Impact

  • In Rural India, Bag valve masks are mostly operated by untrained attendants. They are not aware on how much and how long it has to be used.
  • None of the district hospitals have ventilator support.
  • Big hospitals need In-spire as they get huge inflow of emergency cases every day, for resuscitation, however they cannot use ventilators until the patient is evaluated.
  • Rural Indian Hospitals cannot afford ventilators as they are too expensive and difficult to maintain in rough conditions.

Market Opportunity

  • Need of breathing assistance is high during: Organ failure, Blood infections, Heart problems, Lung problems, Brain trauma
  • Patients in need of medical ventilators who are in transit beds
  • ICUs in need of Backup breathing assistance
